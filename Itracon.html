Dosage & Administration:
• Onychomycosis:
200 mg once daily for 3 months or course (pulse) of 200 mg twice daily for 7 days, subsequent courses repeated after 21 days interval; fingernails 2 courses & toenails 3 courses.
• Vulvovaginal candidiasis:
200 mg twice daily for 1 day
• Tinea pedis:
200 mg once daily for 7 days
• Tinea corporis:
200 mg once daily for 7 days
• Pityriasis versicolor:
200 mg once daily for 7 days
• Tinea manuum:
200mg once daily for 7 days
• Oropharyngeal candidiasis:
100-200 mg twice daily for 2 weeks (continue for another 2 weeks if no response; the higher dose should not be used for longer than 2 weeks if no signs of improvement)
• Systemic candidiasis:
200 mg every 12 hours for 2 days, then 200 mg once daily for max. 12 days
• Histoplasmosis:
200 mg 3 timesa day for 3 days Than 200 mg 1-2 times a day
• Systemic cryptococcosis including cryptococcal meningitis where other antifungal drugs inappropriate or ineffective:
200 mg once daily, dose increased in invasive or disseminated disease and in cryptococcal meningitis, increased to 200 mg twice daily.
• Maintenance in HIV infected patients to prevent relapse of undertying fungal infection and prophylaxis in neutropenia when standard therapy inappropriate:
200 mg once daily, then increased to 200 mg twice daily, dose Increased only if low plasma-itraconazole concentration.
